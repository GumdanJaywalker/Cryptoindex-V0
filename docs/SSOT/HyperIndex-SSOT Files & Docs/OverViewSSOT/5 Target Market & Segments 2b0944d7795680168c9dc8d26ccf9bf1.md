## ðŸ§® 5. The Rationale: Why $4.33B GMV?

*Our projection is conservative when compared to the actual performance of current market leaders.*

We analyzed 5 market segments to set our target:

1. **Major DEXs (Uniswap, Pancake):** Avg Peak ~$224B/month.
2. **Middle DEXs (Meteora, Orca):** Avg Peak ~$41.5B/month.
3. **Aggregators (Jupiter, 1inch):** Avg Peak ~$41.9B/month.
4. **Pump.fun (Direct Trading):** Peak **$18.7B** (excluding launchpad volume).
5. **Trading Bots (Photon, Axiom):** Avg Peak **$6.6B**.

### **Our Positioning Logic**

- **Benchmark:** HyperIndex acts as a **"DEX + Launchpad"** (similar to Pump.fun) but with **value-backed assets** (similar to Trading Platforms).
- **The Target:** Our $4.33B target is:
    - ~45% of Pump.fun&Pump.swapâ€™s 2025OCT Trading Volume Benchmark
    - **~23%** of Pump.fun&Pump.swapâ€™s highest direct trading volume.
    - **~65%** of Photon/Axiom's trading volume.
- **Conclusion:** Targeting $4.33B is highly achievable, positioning us as a **strong mid-tier trading venue** rather than requiring us to flip Uniswap or Pump.fun on Day 1.
